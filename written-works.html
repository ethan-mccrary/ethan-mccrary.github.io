Written Works

I've always had a pretty imaginative brain, so I often find myself writing a lot. I specifically love writing both fantasy and science fiction. I especially have been into writing from the perspective of the SCP Foundation, a wikidot writing community that focuses on a fictional group that secretly secures, contains, and protects anomalous entities and objects.
